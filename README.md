Business Sales Performance Analytics – Power BI
Overview:
This project analyzes business sales data to identify revenue trends, top-performing products, and regional performance. The goal was to build a client-ready dashboard delivering actionable business insights.

Objectives:
- Analyze revenue trends over time
- Identify top-selling products
- Evaluate regional sales performance
- Generate strategic business recommendations

Tools Used:
- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX

Dashboard Preview:

Key Insights:
- Revenue is concentrated in a few top products
- UK dominates total revenue
- Peak sales periods indicate opportunity for time-based promotions
- Premium products generate high revenue despite lower volume

Business Recommendations:
- Ensure stock availability of top products
- Expand marketing beyond UK
- Use seasonal demand analysis
- Improve cross-selling strategies
